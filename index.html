✦ FAQ

Common questions

Is the 14-day trial really free?
▾
Yes, completely free. No credit card is required to start a trial. You won't be charged until the trial ends — and only if you decide to keep Pro. Cancel anytime from Google Play or the App Store.
What happens to my data if I cancel Pro?
▾
Your account stays active on the Free plan. You keep access to all the content you created, but Pro-only features (like live collaboration and premium themes) become unavailable. Your data is never deleted because of a subscription change.
How does live collaboration work?
▾
Create a live session from any to-do list and share a link. Anyone with the link can join and see edits in real time — like Google Docs but for tasks. Split Screen lets two people work on separate lists side by side. Collaboration requires a Pro subscription.
Where is my data stored?
▾
All data is stored securely in the EU (eu-west-1 region) using Convex cloud infrastructure, transmitted over encrypted HTTPS. We do not sell or share your data with advertisers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Is iOS available?
▾
Android is available now on Google Play. iOS (App Store) is coming very soon — currently in review. Stay tuned!
How do I restore my Pro subscription on a new device?
▾
Sign in with the same account you used when you purchased (Google, Apple, or Email — each method is a separate account). Then go to Settings → Restore Purchases inside the app. Your subscription will be restored instantly.
How do I delete my account and data?
▾
Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account inside the app. Everything is permanently deleted within 30 days. Alternatively, email support@lionnotes.online with subject "Delete My Account — Lion's Notes".
