Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

1. Information We Collect

Lion's Notes collects the following data:

  • Account information — name and email address provided via Google, Apple, or email sign-in.
  • User-generated content — to-do lists, checklists, habits, notes, and session data you create.
  • Usage data — app interactions and crash reports (collected anonymously via Sentry to improve stability).
  • Device preferences — theme, language, and settings stored locally on your device.
  • Subscription data — purchase and entitlement status managed by RevenueCat (we do not store payment card details).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to: provide and sync your content across devices, enable live collaboration and shared sessions, maintain and secure your account, diagnose crashes and improve app stability, and process your subscription. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage & Retention

Your content is stored securely on Convex cloud infrastructure hosted in the EU (eu-west-1). All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Anonymized, non-identifiable aggregate statistics may be retained indefinitely.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Clerk — authentication and account management
  • Convex — cloud data storage (EU region)
  • RevenueCat — subscription and payment management
  • Sentry — crash and error reporting (anonymized)
  • Google / Apple — sign-in providers (if you choose OAuth login)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to: access the data we hold about you, correct inaccurate data, delete your account and all associated data, export your content, and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@lionnotes.online.

If you are located in the European Union, you may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (DPA).

6. Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your account directly from the app: Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days. Alternatively, email support@lionnotes.online with subject "Delete My Account — Lion's Notes".

What gets deleted: account, all tasks, habits, notes, settings, session history.
What may be retained: anonymized aggregate statistics only.

7. Children's Privacy

Lion's Notes is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests:
