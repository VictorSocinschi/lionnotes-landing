Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

Data Controller: Victor Socinschi, developer of Lion's Notes.
Contact: support@lionnotes.online

1. Information We Collect

Lion's Notes collects the following data:

  • Account information — name and email address provided via Google, Apple, or email sign-in.
  • User-generated content — to-do lists, checklists, habits, notes, and session data you create.
  • Usage & diagnostic data — app interactions, crash reports, device type, OS version, and app version, collected via Sentry to improve stability. This data may include your IP address.
  • Push notification token — if you enable notifications, your device push token is stored to deliver reminders. You can disable this in your device Settings at any time.
  • Device preferences — theme, language, and settings stored locally on your device.
  • Subscription data — purchase status and entitlement information managed by RevenueCat. We do not store payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to: provide and sync your content across devices, enable live collaboration and shared sessions, maintain and secure your account, diagnose crashes and improve app stability, deliver push notifications you have opted into, and process your subscription.

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

Legal basis for processing (GDPR):

  • Account data & user content — performance of our contract with you (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR)
  • Crash & diagnostic data via Sentry — our legitimate interest in maintaining app stability and security (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR)
  • Subscription management via RevenueCat — performance of our contract with you (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR)
  • Push notifications — your consent, which you may withdraw at any time (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR)

3. Data Storage & Retention

Your content is stored securely on Convex cloud infrastructure hosted in the EU (eu-west-1, Ireland). All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Anonymized, non-identifiable aggregate statistics may be retained indefinitely.

4. Third-Party Services & International Transfers

We use the following trusted services to operate the app. Each has its own privacy policy.

  • Clerk (US) — authentication and account management. Data transfers governed by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).
  • Convex (EU, Ireland) — cloud data storage.
  • RevenueCat (US) — subscription and payment management. Data transfers governed by SCCs.
  • Sentry (US) — crash and error reporting. May collect device info, OS version, app version, and error stack traces. Data transfers governed by SCCs.
  • Google / Apple — sign-in providers (if you choose OAuth login), subject to their respective privacy policies.

Where personal data is transferred outside the EU/EEA, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as approved by the European Commission to ensure an adequate level of protection.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction — request that we limit processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent (e.g. push notifications)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@lionnotes.online. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are located in the European Union, you may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (DPA).

California residents (CCPA): We do not sell your personal data. You have the right to know what data we collect and to request its deletion.

6. Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your account directly from the app: Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days. Alternatively, email support@lionnotes.online with subject "Delete My Account — Lion's Notes".

What gets deleted: account, all tasks, habits, notes, settings, session history.
What may be retained: anonymized aggregate statistics only.

7. Children's Privacy

Lion's Notes is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us immediately at support@lionnotes.online and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
