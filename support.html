🗑️ How to Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your account and all associated data, open the app and go to:

Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account

All your tasks, habits, notes, and settings will be permanently removed within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.

Alternatively, email support@lionnotes.online with the subject "Delete My Account — Lion's Notes" and we'll process it within 30 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?
▾
Go to the Google Play Store → Subscriptions (Android) or App Store → Subscriptions (iOS) and cancel Lion's Notes Pro. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. We do not process cancellations directly.
I was charged but the app still shows Free — what do I do?
▾
Open the app and go to Settings → Restore Purchases — this is the fastest way to recover your Pro access. If that doesn't work, try signing out and back in. Still stuck? Email support@lionnotes.online with your order number and we'll resolve it quickly.
Can I use Lion's Notes on multiple devices?
▾
Yes. Sign in with the same account on any device and your data syncs automatically in real time. One account, all your devices.
I forgot which email I used to sign up — how do I recover my account?
▾
Email us at support@lionnotes.online with any details you remember (approximate sign-up date, device type). We'll help you locate and recover your account.
Is my data safe? Where is it stored?
▾
All data is stored in the EU (eu-west-1) using Convex cloud infrastructure, transmitted over encrypted HTTPS. We never sell your data or use it for advertising. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
The app is crashing — what should I do?
▾
First, try updating to the latest version from Google Play or the App Store. If it still crashes, email support@lionnotes.online with your device model, OS version, and what you were doing when it crashed.
