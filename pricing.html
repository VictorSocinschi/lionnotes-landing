Common questions

Is the 14-day trial really free?
▾
Yes, completely free. No credit card required. You won't be charged until the trial ends — and only if you decide to keep Pro. Cancel anytime from Google Play or the App Store.
What happens to my data if I cancel Pro?
▾
Your account stays on the Free plan. All your content stays intact — Pro-only features (live collaboration, premium themes) become unavailable, but nothing is deleted.
I switched devices — how do I restore my Pro?
▾
Sign in with the same account, then go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Your Pro access restores instantly.
Can I switch between monthly and annual?
▾
Yes — change your billing period any time from Google Play or App Store subscription settings. The change takes effect at the next renewal.
Do you offer refunds?
▾
Refunds are handled by Google Play or the App Store per their policies. If you have an issue, email support@lionnotes.online and we'll help.
