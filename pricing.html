Common questions

Is the 14-day trial really free?
▾
Yes, completely free. No credit card is required to start a trial. You won't be charged until the trial ends — and only if you decide to keep Pro. Cancel anytime from Google Play or the App Store.
What happens to my data if I cancel Pro?
▾
Your account stays active on the Free plan. You keep access to all the content you created, but Pro-only features (like live collaboration and premium themes) become unavailable. Your data is never deleted because of a subscription change.
Can I switch between monthly and annual?
▾
Yes. You can change your billing period from the Google Play or App Store subscription settings. The change takes effect at the start of your next billing period.
I switched devices — how do I restore my Pro?
▾
Sign in with the same account you used to purchase, then go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Your Pro access will be restored instantly.
Do you offer refunds?
▾
Refunds are handled by Google Play or the App Store according to their refund policies. We do not process refunds directly. If you have an issue, contact us at support@lionnotes.online and we'll do our best to help.
